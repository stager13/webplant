This application requires canvas support

Drag with middle mouse button pressed to rotate camera.
Drag with Shift+middle mouse button to move camera.
Use mouse wheel to zoom-in/zoom-out.
Use numpad keys 7,1,3 to switch to "Top","Front" or "Side" view.
Use numpad keys 4,6,2,8 to rotate camera.
Click on "Trunk","Branch1" etc label on the right to choose branch group to edit.
